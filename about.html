About the Author

Hello! My name is Tatiana, and I am the author of this blog page! I am a Junior at Michigan State University, and am currently pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science and a B.A. in Information Science. After graduation I am hoping to become a Full-Stack Software Developer.

In my free time, I love to read, cook, play my switch, and hang out with my friends. Additionally, I am involved in several organizations on campus:

  • MSU Women In Computing
  • Women In Engineering K-12 Outreach Office
  • Phi Sigma Rho

I love to travel; however, that is not quite as easy to do as my other interests. I have always wanted to explore new places and experience new cultures. I hope to be able to travel a lot of the world in the future.
